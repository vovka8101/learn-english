peach = персик = a round fruit with sweet yellow flesh that has a lot of juice, a slightly furry red and yellow skin, and a large seed in its centre: = The peach tree has an excellent blossom this year. / There's nothing better than a nice juicy peach!
grapes = виноград = a small, round, purple or pale green fruit that you can eat or make into wine: = The grapes are made into wine. / The wine is made from organically grown grapes.
jam = варення = a sweet, soft food made by cooking fruit with sugar to preserve it. It is eaten on bread: = He spread some strawberry jam on his toast. / The recipe uses a whole jarful/two jarfuls of jam.
strawberry = полуниця = a small juicy red fruit that has small brown seeds on its surface, or the plant with white flowers on which this fruit grows: = I've made a strawberry jelly for the children's tea. / I only had a strawberry yoghurt for breakfast.
